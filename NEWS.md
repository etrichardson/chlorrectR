## chlorrectR Version 0.1.0
 In this original version, the function correct_fchl is created. correct_fchl allows users to input raw or pre-temperature corrected chlorophyll fluorescence data from one of the following fluorometers:

 - YSI EXO2
 - YSI 6600
 - bbe Moldaenke Fluoroprobe
 - Seabird WETStar

correct_fchl uses specific intrumentation correction cuidance from Watras (2017) and Richardson (2025) to correct chlorophyll fluorescence data to make it closer to laboratory-derived chlorohyll-a concentration data, and more interoperable with each brand listed above.

Richardson, E.T., Kraus, T.E.C., Sturgeon, C.L., O'Donnell, K. and Bergamaschi, B.A. (2025), Identifying conditions associated with outliers produced by three different chlorophyll fluorometers: A comparison of instrumentation and development of correction formulae. Limnol Oceanogr Methods, 23: 673-687. https://doi.org/10.1002/lom3.10705.

Watras, C.J., Morrison, K.A., Rubsam, J.L., Hanson, P.C., Watras, A.J., LaLiberte, G.D. and Milewski, P. (2017), A temperature compensation method for chlorophyll and phycocyanin fluorescence sensors in freshwater. Limnol. Oceanogr. Methods, 15: 642-652. https://doi.org/10.1002/lom3.10188.
